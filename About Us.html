ABOUT US

Nestled in the vibrant heart of West Fairview, Grind & Dose is
more than just a coffee shop; it’s an experience for coffee
enthusiasts and casual drinkers alike. As a proud purveyor of
third-wave coffee, Grind & Dose redefines what it means to
enjoy a classic brew by adding its own unique touch. Each cup is
thoughtfully prepared, with the shop making its own syrups,
carefully balancing flavors, and tweaking standard recipes to
deliver a familiar selection with a refreshingly distinct twist.

Grind & Dose’s dedication to quality extends beyond coffee. The
menu features a handpicked selection of pastries and small bites
that perfectly complement the beverage offerings. Every
ingredient is sourced with care, and each item is crafted to
highlight the natural flavors and textures that elevate both the
coffee and the food.

Whether you're there to work, catch up with friends, or simply
unwind, the warm, inviting atmosphere of Grind & Dose serves as
the perfect backdrop. The skilled baristas are passionate about
creating the best possible experience for each guest, guiding
them through the menu, recommending pairings, and sharing the
story behind every brew. At Grind & Dose, every visit is a chance
to explore coffee on a deeper level, savoring quality, creativity,
and community in every sip.

Meet The Founder

ASHA LOVILLA

Ms. Asha Lovilla, the founder of Grind & Dose, is a 25-year-old entrepreneur and a graduate of the
University of Santo Tomas, where she earned a Bachelor of Science in Culinary Entrepreneurship
from the College of Tourism and Hospitality Management.

Her passion for the coffee industry and her experience as a bartender and consultant in various
coffee shops shaped her vision for Grind & Dose, a cafe located in West Fairview.

As the current General Manager, Ms. Lovilla is hands-on with daily operations, focusing on
providing quality coffee and creating a welcoming atmosphere for her customers. She is dedicated
to sharing her love for coffee with thse community, ensuring that Grind & Dose stands out as a
unique space for coffee enthusiasts.

Her academic background and industry experience contribute to her ability to manage and grow
the business effectively.
